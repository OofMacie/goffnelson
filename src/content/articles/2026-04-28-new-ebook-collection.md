Great news for digital readers! Goff Nelson Memorial Library has added **500 new e-book titles** to our Libby collection, available immediately to all cardholders at no cost.

## What's New

- **150 bestselling fiction titles** including recent releases
- **200 nonfiction titles** covering history, science, cooking, and more
- **150 children's and young adult titles**

## How to Access

1. Download the **Libby app** on your phone or tablet
2. Search for "Goff Nelson Memorial Library"
3. Sign in with your library card number
4. Start reading!

No waiting lists on most new titles. Questions? Ask at the circulation desk or call us during library hours.
